Geography of Housing Prices in California

This interactive map visualizes a change in median housing prices over the last 9 years for major cities in California. The orange circles represent a yearly change in housing prices for those cities. Use the slider to update years, and hover over individual cities to explore further. The legend shows minimum, maximum, and mean housing prices in the state during the selceted year.

Created with: JavaScript, jQuery, Leaflet, and AJAX.


Source: California Association of Realtors
